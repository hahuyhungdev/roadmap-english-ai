### 1b. Synonyms theo sắc thái

Đừng dùng mãi một từ. Nhưng cũng đừng thay bừa — mỗi từ có sắc thái riêng.

**"Use" — dùng:**
- use (trung tính, bình thường)
- utilise (trang trọng hơn, có chủ đích)
- rely on (phụ thuộc vào)
- harness (khai thác tối đa tiềm năng) → *harness the power of AI*
- adopt (bắt đầu dùng một cái gì mới) → *adopt new technology*

**"Important" — quan trọng:**
- important (trung tính)
- crucial / vital (rất quan trọng, thiếu là không được)
- significant (đáng kể, có ảnh hưởng)
- indispensable (không thể thiếu)
- pivotal (mang tính bước ngoặt)

**"Bad effect" — ảnh hưởng tiêu cực:**
- bad effect (❌ quá đơn giản, tránh dùng)
- drawback / downside (nhược điểm)
- concern (mối lo ngại)
- threat (mối đe dọa)
- consequence (hậu quả)
- pitfall (cạm bẫy, rủi ro tiềm ẩn)

**"Change" — thay đổi:**
- change (trung tính)
- transform (thay đổi hoàn toàn)
- reshape (định hình lại)
- revolutionise (cách mạng hóa)
- disrupt (phá vỡ cái cũ theo cách đột ngột)

- **access** the internet / information / services

- **harness** the power of AI / technology
- **raise** concerns about privacy / screen time
- **pose** a threat to job security / privacy
- **bridge** the digital divide
- **revolutionise** the way we communicate / work / learn

- **keep up with** technological change

- **rapid** technological advancement

- **widespread** internet access / use of smartphones
- **significant** impact on society / daily life
- **emerging** technologies
- **unprecedented** levels of connectivity
- **excessive** screen time / use of social media

### Adverb + Adjective

- **increasingly** dependent on smartphones
- **widely** accessible / available / used

## PHẦN 5 — GRAMMAR STRUCTURES

Không học tất cả grammar — chỉ học 5 cấu trúc hiệu quả nhất cho topic này ở band 5→6+.

### Cấu trúc 1 — Conditional Type 2 (Giả thuyết hiện tại)

Dùng khi: đề xuất giải pháp, nói về điều chưa xảy ra.

Công thức: *If + S + V2, S + would/could + V*

Ví dụ trong topic:
- *If people spent less time on social media, they would feel less anxious.*
- *If governments invested more in digital infrastructure, more people could access the internet.*
- *If I didn't have my phone, I would probably feel completely lost.*

Trước khi học → Sau khi học:
- ❌ *Without phone, I feel lost* → ✅ *If I didn't have my phone, I would feel completely lost.*

---

### Cấu trúc 2 — Passive Voice (Câu bị động)

Dùng khi: nói về tác động của technology lên xã hội một cách khách quan.

Công thức: *S + is/are/has been + V3*

Ví dụ:
- *Personal data is being collected and sold without users' knowledge.*
- *Millions of jobs have been replaced by automated systems.*
- *Technology is increasingly being used in classrooms around the world.*

---

### Cấu trúc 3 — Concession Clause (Nhượng bộ)

Dùng khi: thể hiện tư duy cân bằng hai phía — rất quan trọng cho Part 3.

Công thức: *Although/Even though/Despite the fact that + clause, + main clause*

Ví dụ:
- *Although technology has created millions of new jobs, it has simultaneously eliminated many traditional ones.*
- *Even though social media connects people globally, it can also lead to feelings of isolation.*
- *Despite the fact that smartphones are incredibly useful, excessive use can harm mental health.*

---

### Cấu trúc 4 — Relative Clause (Mệnh đề quan hệ)

Dùng khi: giải thích, định nghĩa, mô tả — thay vì dùng 2 câu ngắn.

Công thức: *Noun + which/who/where/that + clause*

Ví dụ:
- *The digital divide, which refers to the gap between those with and without internet access, remains a serious issue.*
- *Social media platforms, which were originally designed to connect people, have become sources of misinformation.*
- *I use an app that tracks my daily screen time.*

---

### Cấu trúc 5 — Tendency (Xu hướng — rất tự nhiên trong Speaking)

Dùng khi: nói về hành vi, thói quen xã hội — không bị áp đặt như "people always".

Công thức: *People tend to / are likely to / have a tendency to + V*

Ví dụ:
- *Younger generations tend to rely more heavily on technology than older people.*
- *People are likely to feel anxious if they can't access the internet.*
- *We have a tendency to underestimate how much time we spend online.*

**Bài tập — Viết lại câu:**

Dùng đúng cấu trúc được chỉ định:

1. "Technology changes fast. It is hard to follow." → Dùng Relative Clause
2. "Social media is good. But it causes addiction." → Dùng Concession Clause
3. "Young people use phones too much." → Dùng Tendency

*(Gợi ý: 1- Technology, which changes very fast, is hard to keep up with. / 2- Although social media has its benefits, it can lead to addiction. / 3- Young people tend to use their phones excessively.)*

## PHẦN 6 — DISCOURSE MARKERS

Đây là thứ tạo ra "flow" — giám khảo nghe 30 giây đầu đã biết bạn ở band mấy.

| Chức năng | Markers | Dùng ở đâu |
|---|---|---|
| Thêm ý | Furthermore / Moreover / What's more / On top of that | Speaking & Writing |
| Tương phản | However / Nevertheless / That said / On the other hand | Cả hai |
| Kết quả | As a result / Consequently / This has led to | Cả hai |
| Ví dụ | For instance / To illustrate / A case in point is | Cả hai |
| Nhấn mạnh | In particular / Notably / Above all | Cả hai |
| Tổng kết | On balance / All things considered / Ultimately | Cả hai |
| Chỉ dùng Speaking | Well / To be honest / I mean / You know what I mean | Speaking only |
| Chỉ dùng Writing | Furthermore / Hence / Thus | Writing only |

**Bài tập — Đọc đoạn sau và nhận ra discourse markers:**

*"Technology has transformed the way we communicate. Furthermore, it has made information more accessible than ever before. However, this has also led to serious concerns about misinformation. To illustrate, fake news spreads far faster on social media than in traditional media. On balance, I believe the benefits outweigh the drawbacks, provided we use technology responsibly."*

Hỏi: Đoạn này dùng bao nhiêu discourse markers? Chúng thuộc nhóm chức năng nào?

*(Tự kiểm: Furthermore-thêm ý / However-tương phản / this has led to-kết quả / To illustrate-ví dụ / On balance-tổng kết)*

## PHẦN 7 — IDEAS BANK

Mỗi ý theo công thức **Point → Explanation → Example (PEE)**. Đọc hiểu, không học thuộc — sau đó tự nói lại bằng lời của bạn.

### Lợi ích

**Ý 1 — Kết nối toàn cầu**
Point: Technology has brought people closer together regardless of distance.
Explanation: With smartphones and the internet, people can communicate instantly with anyone in the world.
Example: For instance, video calling apps like Zoom allowed families to stay connected during the pandemic when travel was impossible.

**Ý 2 — Tiếp cận giáo dục**
Point: Technology has democratised access to education.
Explanation: Online platforms allow people in remote areas to learn from world-class teachers without leaving home.
Example: A student in a rural village in Vietnam can now access the same quality of education as someone in Hanoi, simply through a smartphone.

**Ý 3 — Tăng năng suất**
Point: Technology has significantly boosted productivity in the workplace.
Explanation: Automated tools handle repetitive tasks, freeing humans to focus on creative and strategic work.
Example: In my own experience, using productivity apps helps me manage my time far more efficiently than before.

**Ý 4 — Y tế**
Point: Technology has revolutionised healthcare.
Explanation: AI can now diagnose diseases with remarkable accuracy, and telemedicine allows patients to consult doctors remotely.
Example: During the COVID-19 pandemic, remote consultations helped millions of people receive medical advice safely.

---

### Hạn chế

**Ý 1 — Phụ thuộc quá mức**
Point: One of the biggest concerns is our growing overdependence on technology.
Explanation: As we rely more heavily on devices for even basic tasks, we risk losing essential skills like navigation, mental arithmetic, and face-to-face communication.
Example: Many young people today cannot read a physical map — a skill that was considered basic just twenty years ago.

**Ý 2 — Sức khỏe tâm thần**
Point: Excessive use of social media has been linked to rising rates of anxiety and depression.
Explanation: Constant exposure to curated, idealised images of others' lives creates unrealistic comparisons and feelings of inadequacy.
Example: Research has shown that teenagers who spend more than three hours a day on social media are significantly more likely to experience mental health issues.

**Ý 3 — Khoảng cách số**
Point: Technology has widened the gap between the rich and the poor.
Explanation: Those who cannot afford devices or internet access are left behind in an increasingly digital world.
Example: During the pandemic, millions of children in developing countries could not attend online classes simply because they lacked a smartphone or stable internet.

**Ý 4 — Mất việc làm**
Point: Automation is threatening millions of jobs worldwide.
Explanation: As machines become capable of performing tasks previously done by humans, many workers — particularly in manufacturing and data entry — face unemployment.
Example: It is estimated that up to 30% of current jobs could be automated within the next two decades.

---

### Giải pháp (cho Part 3)

**Giải pháp 1:** Governments should invest in digital literacy programmes to ensure people use technology safely and responsibly.

**Giải pháp 2:** Tech companies need to be more strictly regulated in terms of data privacy and content moderation.

**Giải pháp 3:** Individuals can set personal boundaries — such as phone-free hours or screen time limits — to maintain a healthy relationship with technology.

## PHẦN 8 — SPEAKING PRACTICE

### Part 1 — Short Answers

**Q: Do you use technology a lot in your daily life?**

Band 6 answer:
*"Yes, I use technology every day. I use my phone for many things like social media, work and entertainment. I think technology is very useful."*

Band 7 answer:
*"Honestly, yes — I'd say I'm pretty reliant on it. I use my smartphone for almost everything, from keeping up with the news to managing my work schedule. Having said that, I do try to cut down on screen time in the evenings because I've noticed it affects my sleep quite badly."*

Tại sao Band 7 tốt hơn: dùng collocation (reliant on, keeping up with, cut down on), có discourse marker (Having said that), có chi tiết cụ thể (evenings, affects my sleep), không bắt đầu bằng "Yes I use..."

---

**Q: What kind of technology do you use most?**

Band 6 answer:
*"I use my smartphone most. I use it for many things every day."*

Band 7 answer:
*"Without a doubt, my smartphone is the device I rely on most heavily. It's become so deeply integrated into my daily life that I use it for everything from setting my morning alarm to communicating with colleagues and winding down with a podcast before bed."*

---

**Q: Do you think people rely on technology too much?**

Band 6 answer:
*"Yes, I think people depend on technology too much. It's not good for us."*

Band 7 answer:
*"To be honest, I think there's a growing concern about this. While technology has undeniably made our lives more convenient, many people — myself included — have become so reliant on their devices that we struggle to function without them. That being said, I think it comes down to personal responsibility rather than technology itself being the problem."*

### Part 2 — Cue Card

**Describe a piece of technology that you find useful.**
You should say:
- What it is
- How often you use it
- What you use it for
- And explain why you find it so useful

**Dàn ý 1 phút (viết bullet points như thế này):**
- What: smartphone / laptop → laptop
- How often: every single day, for hours
- What for: work, research, entertainment, communication
- Why useful: all in one, portable, boosts productivity, can't imagine without it
- Personal story: lockdown, worked from home, laptop was lifeline
- Feeling: grateful, but also aware of overdependence

**Bài nói mẫu (2 phút):**

*"The piece of technology I'd like to talk about is my laptop. I've had it for about three years now, and I'd say it's become absolutely indispensable to my daily life.*

*I use it every single day — easily four to six hours on weekdays, and sometimes even more on weekends if I have projects to work on. I use it for pretty much everything: managing work tasks, doing research, attending online meetings, and winding down with a film in the evening.*

*What I find particularly useful about it is that it combines everything I need in one portable device. Before I had a good laptop, I had to rely on multiple devices and was constantly switching between them, which was incredibly inefficient. Now I can do everything in one place, which has significantly boosted my productivity.*

*I remember during the COVID lockdown, my laptop was essentially my lifeline. Everything — work, communication with friends and family, even grocery shopping — went through that one device. It really made me appreciate how much modern technology can support us during difficult times.*

*Having said that, I've also become more aware of how heavily I rely on it. Sometimes I catch myself reaching for it out of habit rather than necessity, which is something I'm genuinely trying to address. But overall, I think it's a tool that, when used responsibly, genuinely improves quality of life.*"*

**Phân tích bài nói:**
- absolutely indispensable → collocation + vocab upgrade
- What I find particularly useful → chunk mở đầu nhấn mạnh
- significantly boosted my productivity → collocation mạnh
- Having said that → discourse marker contrast
- when used responsibly → passive clause tự nhiên

---

### Part 3 — Discussion

**Q: Do you think technology will replace human workers in the future?**

*"That's a question that generates a lot of debate, and I can see both sides. On one hand, it's undeniable that automation has already phased out a significant number of manual and repetitive jobs. On the other hand, I think it's an oversimplification to say technology will replace humans entirely.*

*What's more likely, in my view, is that it will reshape the workforce rather than eliminate it — creating new roles that we can't even imagine yet, much like the Industrial Revolution did. Having said that, I do think governments need to urgently look into retraining programmes to ensure that workers who are displaced by automation aren't simply left behind.*

*So on balance, I'm cautiously optimistic — but I think the transition needs to be managed very carefully."*

---

**Q: Is the internet making people more or less social?**

*"That's an interesting one because I think the answer is — both, actually. The internet has, without question, made it easier to stay connected with people across the world. I can keep in touch with friends who've moved abroad without any effort at all.*

*However, there's a growing body of research suggesting that heavy social media use is actually leading to increased loneliness and social isolation, particularly among young people. The concern is that online interaction — however convenient — lacks the depth and emotional richness of face-to-face communication.*

*So I'd say the internet has made us more broadly connected but potentially less deeply social. It depends heavily on how consciously we choose to use it."*

---

**Q: Should children be taught how to use technology at school?**

*"Absolutely — I think digital literacy is just as important as reading and writing in today's world. Children who grow up without understanding how to navigate the digital landscape are going to be at a serious disadvantage in both higher education and the workplace.*

*That said, there's an important distinction between teaching children to use technology productively and simply giving them unrestricted access. What schools should focus on is teaching critical thinking around technology — how to evaluate information online, how to stay safe, and how to use digital tools effectively rather than passively.*

*So yes, technology education is essential, but it needs to be done thoughtfully."*

---

- What are the dangers of children spending too much time on screens?

### Mini Mock — Tự luyện cuối bài

Ghi âm bản thân trả lời toàn bộ phần này. Không xem note. Sau đó tự nghe lại và đánh dấu xem mình đã dùng được bao nhiêu thứ đã học.

**Part 1:** Trả lời 3 câu sau, mỗi câu 2–3 câu:
1. How often do you use the internet?
2. Do you think technology has made life better or worse?
3. What technology do you wish you could use more?

**Part 2:** Nói 2 phút về cue card sau:
*Describe a time when technology helped you solve a problem.*
(1 phút chuẩn bị, 2 phút nói)

**Part 3:** Trả lời 2 câu sau, mỗi câu 40–60 giây:
1. Do you think artificial intelligence is more of a benefit or a threat to society?
2. How do you think technology will change education in the next 20 years?
