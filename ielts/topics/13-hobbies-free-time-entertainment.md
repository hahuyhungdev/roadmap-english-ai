## Part 2 - Vocabulary In Context

### 2a. Word Families

1. **relax / relaxation / relaxing**
   - Meaning: rest and feel less stressed.
   - Natural: Listening to music is relaxing after a long day.
   - IT/pro: After debugging for hours, I need something relaxing to clear my head.
   - Mistake: Avoid `make me relaxable`; say `it helps me relax`.

2. **enjoy / enjoyment / enjoyable**
   - Meaning: like doing something.
   - Natural: I enjoy activities that don't require too much planning.
   - IT/pro: I enjoy small hobbies that help me disconnect from screens.
   - Mistake: Avoid `I enjoy to watch`; say `I enjoy watching`.

3. **entertain / entertainment / entertaining**
   - Meaning: activities that amuse or interest people.
   - Natural: Short videos are entertaining, but they can waste time.
   - IT/pro: Entertainment apps are designed to keep users engaged.
   - Mistake: Avoid `entertainment myself`; say `entertain myself`.

4. **recharge / recharge / recharged**
   - Meaning: get energy back.
   - Natural: A quiet evening helps me recharge.
   - IT/pro: I need to recharge after intense meetings or deadlines.
   - Mistake: Avoid `charge my energy`; say `recharge my energy`.

5. **interest / interested / interesting**
   - Meaning: curiosity or enjoyment.
   - Natural: I'm interested in activities that combine learning and fun.
   - IT/pro: I'm interested in tech videos, but I try not to turn all free time into work.
   - Mistake: Avoid `I'm interesting in`; say `I'm interested in`.

6. **habit / habitual / regularly**
   - Meaning: something you do often.
   - Natural: A hobby becomes more useful when it becomes a regular habit.
   - IT/pro: Regular exercise or reading can support long-term focus.
   - Mistake: Avoid `I have habit watch movie`; say `I have a habit of watching movies`.

7. **balance / balanced / imbalance**
   - Meaning: enough of different things, not too much of one.
   - Natural: Free time helps me keep a better balance between work and life.
   - IT/pro: If I only work and scroll, my life feels out of balance.
   - Mistake: Avoid `balance work and relax`; say `balance work and relaxation`.

8. **create / creative / creativity**
   - Meaning: make something or use imagination.
   - Natural: Creative hobbies can be refreshing because they use a different part of the mind.
   - IT/pro: Even frontend work has a creative side, so I enjoy hobbies that train taste and attention.
   - Mistake: Avoid `create my stress down`; say `reduce my stress`.

### 2e. Common Vietnamese Learner Mistakes

```text
Mistake: I usually relax myself by music.
Better: I usually relax by listening to music.
Why: Use "relax by + V-ing".
```

```text
Mistake: I enjoy to play games.
Better: I enjoy playing games.
Why: "Enjoy" is followed by V-ing.
```

```text
Mistake: In free time, I watch movie.
Better: In my free time, I watch movies.
Why: Use "my free time" and plural "movies" for general habit.
```

```text
Mistake: It makes me reduce stress.
Better: It helps me reduce stress. / It reduces my stress.
Why: "Help me + verb" is more natural.
```

```text
Mistake: I'm interesting in music.
Better: I'm interested in music.
Why: "Interested" describes your feeling; "interesting" describes the thing.
```

```text
Mistake: I have many free times.
Better: I have a lot of free time.
Why: "Free time" is uncountable.
```

```text
Mistake: I play sport.
Better: I play sports. / I do exercise.
Why: Use "sports" generally or "exercise" for physical activity.
```

```text
Mistake: I want to have relax.
Better: I wanna relax. / I want to relax.
Why: Use "relax" as a verb.
```

Weak answer:



## Part 4 - Speaking Patterns, Grammar & Expression Tips

### Pattern 1: Habit And Purpose

```text
I usually listen to music after work because it helps me clear my head.
```

Use it for:

```text
I usually + activity + because it helps me...
```

### Pattern 2: Preference With Reason

```text
I prefer low-pressure activities because my work already requires a lot of focus.
```

Use it for:

```text
I prefer + noun/V-ing + because...
```

### Pattern 3: Balanced Entertainment Answer

```text
Short videos are entertaining, but if I watch them for too long, I feel more tired.
```

Use it for:

```text
X is entertaining, but if..., ...
```

### Pattern 4: Recent Habit

```text
Recently, I've been trying to spend less time scrolling and more time walking.
```

Use it for:

```text
Recently, I've been trying to spend less time... and more time...
```

### Grammar Focus: Gerunds After Enjoy, Prefer, Avoid

Use V-ing after these verbs:

```text
I enjoy watching movies.
I prefer walking alone.
I avoid scrolling too much before bed.
```

Common mistake:

```text
Avoid: I enjoy to listen music.
Better: I enjoy listening to music.
```

### Expression Tips

- Don't list hobbies only; explain what each activity gives you.
- Use `helps me + verb`: helps me relax, helps me focus, helps me sleep.
- Use `take my mind off work` for mental rest.
- Mention screen balance if you're an IT learner.
- Don't make hobbies sound like another productivity project.

### Part 2 Cue Card

```text
Describe a hobby or free-time activity you enjoy.

You should say:
- what the activity is
- when you usually do it
- who you do it with
- and explain why you enjoy it
```

### Model Answer

```text
I'd like to talk about walking, which sounds very simple, but it's one of the free-time activities I enjoy the most.

I usually go for a walk in the evening, especially after a long day of working on my laptop. I don't always walk with someone. Sometimes I go alone because I need quiet time, but sometimes I walk with a friend and just have a casual conversation.

The reason I enjoy it is that it helps me disconnect from screens. As a developer, I spend a lot of time reading code, checking messages, and looking at documentation, so my brain can feel overloaded. Walking is a low-pressure activity. I don't need to achieve anything. I just move, breathe, and let my mind slow down.

Another reason is that walking helps me process ideas naturally. Sometimes when I'm stuck with a problem, I stop forcing it, go outside, and the solution becomes clearer later. So for me, walking isn't a serious hobby, but it's a very useful way to recharge.
```
